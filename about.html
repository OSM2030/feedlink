About FeedLink

At FeedLink, we believe that no food should ever go to waste. Our platform connects donors with donees to redistribute excess food, reducing food waste and addressing hunger. Whether you're an individual, organization, or event host, FeedLink provides an easy way to donate leftover food to people who need it most.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable platform that fosters collaboration between communities to combat food wastage and hunger. We strive to bridge the gap between food abundance and scarcity by facilitating easy connections between donors and donees.

How It Works

Register as a Donor

Sign up and become a part of the FeedLink community to start donating excess food.

Locate a Donee

Search for donees in your area who need the food you want to donate.

Contact the Donee

Reach out to the donee and arrange the donation. Together, we can make a difference!
